Projects

Predicting Flower Prices at OCS (Ontario Cannabis Store)

In this machine learning project, I used Selenium in Python to scrape the OCS website to extract the product info for over 300 cannabis strains. After creating graphs and calculating statistics to identify the relationships among the variables (e.g. price, grams, THC/CBD amounts), I implemented Linear, Lasso, and Random Forest (RF) Regressions to estimate flower prices.

Random Forest outperformed the other two models with a mean absolute error (MAE) of 0.85.
